# Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance and customer behavior using key performance indicators (KPIs) and business questions.

---

## 📊 KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)

- **Total Orders** – Total number of orders placed  
- **Total Quantity** – Total number of items sold  
- **Total Amount** – Total revenue generated  
- **Average Rating** – Average customer satisfaction rating  
- **Average Days to Deliver** – Average delivery time for orders  

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

### 📈 Sales Trends
- Trend in the last 13 weeks  

### 🛒 Customer Buying Behavior
- How customers prefer to buy  
- How many products they purchase  

### 🏆 Product Insights
- Which products are most popular  

### 👥 Customer Demographics
- Overall gender distribution  
- Where customers are located  

### 🚚 Delivery Performance
- How long it takes to ship orders  

### 😊 Customer Satisfaction
- How happy customers are (based on ratings)  

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to:
- Understand customer behavior  
- Identify top-performing products  
- Monitor delivery performance  
- Improve overall business decision-making  

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This analysis helps transform raw data into actionable insights, enabling better strategic decisions and improved customer experience.
